FAQ

How do I request artwork?
After adding me, describe to me exactly what you want - that means positioning, effects, and overall goal of the animation.

How much do you charge?
I am currently charging 2 to 4 CS:GO keys / $4 to $9. The price is determined by the difficulty of the piece. The lowest price is for the templates I made that I reuse if the client desires.

How will I receive my artwork?
After finishing the animation, I will send you a watermarked version and continue changing it to your liking. Once you are satisfied, I will accept the payment via steam or paypal then send you the unwatermarked version for download.
